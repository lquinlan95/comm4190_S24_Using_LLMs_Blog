I just finished a yoga retreat in the Amazon Jungle, and I want to commemorate the experience. I also thought this could be a great opportunity to test the image generation abilities of various LLMs.

Commemorating a positive experience

I was interested in trying multiple different LLMs to determine which could perform this task best. Of course, I wanted to try ChatGPT, Gemini, and CoPilot. I also wanted to try Tattoo GPT.

For those of you who don’t know, ChatGPT has specific GPTs that have been trained to complete more specific tasks that the general ChatGPT system. One of these public GPTs available for use is called Tattoo GPT. I thought this would be the perfect opportunity to test out its capabilities.

I asked the same request of ChatGPT, Tattoo GPT, Gemini, and CoPilot: ” I just finished a yoga and meditation retreat, and I am feeling very centered and connected to nature. The retreat was in the amazon jungle and was hosted by shipibo shamans. Can you help me design a simple and elegant tattoo to remember the experience”
